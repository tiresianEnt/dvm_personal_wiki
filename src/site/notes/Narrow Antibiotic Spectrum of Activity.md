#### Nature
The [[Antibiotics\|antibiotic]] can only target either [[Gram Negative\|Gram Negative]] or [[Gram Positive\|Gram Positive]] bacteria.

#### Examples
[[Penicillin\|Penicillin]] is an example of a narrow antibiotic.
